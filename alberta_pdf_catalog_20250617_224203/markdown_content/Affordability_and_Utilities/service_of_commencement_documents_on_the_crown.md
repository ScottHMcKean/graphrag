#  Service of commencement documents on the Crown

There are 4 ways to serve commencement documents on the Crown. 

## Email

Certain commencement documents can be served by email on His Majesty the King in right of Alberta, the Premier of Alberta, any provincial minister, and the director of SafeRoads Alberta.

Email: [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#244e57430a574156524d47414c494f64434b520a45460a4745)

## Mail, fax or delivery

Hours: 8:15 am to 12 pm and 1 pm to 4:30 pm (open Monday to Friday, closed statutory holidays)
